How does it work?

Now everyone can enjoy true luxury!
The LuxuryHotelGiftVoucher is a special, lavish gift for a maximum price of EUR185. This voucher allows you and your partner (or a friend or family member) to enjoy an exclusive package deal in an exquisite hotel in the Netherlands, Germany, Belgium, France, Austria, Slovenia or Portugal. It is two for the price of one, which means your companion stays for free!

Specially selected hotels and unique package dealsEvery hotel affiliated with LuxuryHotelCompany offers something special. One may have excellent wellness facilities, while another may be famous for its outstanding golf course.

Affiliated hotels may be situated in bustling city centres, or hidden away in breathtaking natural environments. The choice is entirely up to you! The unique package deals that have been exclusively put together for LuxuryHotelCompany are broken down in the following categories: wellness, golfing, kids, beauty treatments, culinary, hiking, fresh sea air, cycling or city. Furthermore, you can book inexpensive VIP packages at LuxuryHotelCompany: these packages offer perks and treatments that make your stay even more enjoyable.

LuxuryHotelGiftVoucher required
You can find all hotels and their corresponding package deals at www.luxuryhotelcompany.com. Here you can sign in with the unique code on your voucher. The voucher is required when booking a room. You are to hand it in when checking in at the hotel.   

With the LuxuryHotelGiftVoucher you are in possession of a fantastic gift. We hope to welcome you soon to one of the luxurious hotels, and we wish you and your companion an unforgettable stay in advance!
