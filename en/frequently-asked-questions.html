Frequently asked questions

  • How do I log in to your website?

    Go to www.luxuryhotelcompany.com for the login screen. Here you can log in with your voucher code and your email. Once you are logged in, you can view all affiliated hotels and their corresponding package deals.

  • Where can I find a list of hotels affiliated with you?

    Go to www.luxuryhotelcompany.com. After you have logged in with your voucher code and your email, you are on our main site with a list of all hotels and corresponding package deals.

  • How is the value of the packages determined?

    LuxuryHotelCompany offers over 200 hotel packages, which are all different and may consist of 1, 2 or 3 nights complemented by for instance breakfast, dinner, bicycle rental, free use of wellness facilities and many other options. De composition of these extras is determined together with the hotel.

    The advantage you receive is based on averages, calculated over an entire year. So during the peak or low season, your advantage could deviate from that average. This means that sometimes your advantage will be much larger than indicated on www.luxuryhotelcompany.com, while at other times it could be a little less. This depends on the different rates the relevant hotel charges during the course of the year.

  • When is the hotel of my choice available?

    When you fill out the booking form, there is a schedule to the right that lists arrivals and departures. It shows the availability of the hotels for the next three months. The bookings made on our website are always on the request, the hotel can still refuse the bookings.

  • How can I view the hotels affiliated with LuxuryHotelCompany if I do not have a voucher?

    It is not possible to view all affiliated hotels without a voucher. You have to log in to www.luxuryhotelcompany.com. You can, however, view a number of recommended package deal examples at www.lhcv.nl. The hotels listed are comparable to the ones affiliated with us.

  • Can I stay at a hotel by myself?

    You cannot stay at hotels without a companion. All package deals are put together for two people. Without a companion, you are not eligible for the discount.

  • How many affiliated hotels do you have in the Netherlands and in other countries?

    We have a total of over 200 affiliated hotels in eight different European countries. Below you can see how the hotels are divided between countries.

  • Where can I cancel my booking? Do I need to contact you or the hotel?

    You can contact us to cancel your booking. You can mail or call us, and we will contact the hotel and send you a confirmation of the annulment. Depending on the cancellation policy of the hotel cancellation fees can be charged.

  • How much do I eventually pay when checking in at the hotel?

    The first person pays the package price and booking price when checking in, as specified on the website. When you hand in your signed voucher, your companion stays for free. There is no restitution or other compensation for package deals under EUR185. If the theme package deal for the hotel of your choice costs less than EUR185, the lower sum is the total worth of your companion’s LuxuryHotelGiftVoucher. 

     

    An example: your chosen accommodation offers a theme package deal worth EUR154 for three nights. You pay EUR154 and your companion hands in the LuxuryHotelGiftVoucher for his or her free stay. The total worth of the LuxuryHotelGiftVoucher for this reservation is EUR154. EUR185 minus EUR154 is EUR31. You will not receive the difference of EUR31. When you book a package deal for EUR185, the total discount for your companion is EUR185 as well.

     
  • Can I extend my voucher?

    You can call or mail us to extend your voucher. However, we will only extend it once.

  • I have booked, but have yet to receive a confirmation.

    You can contact us about this. We will check the status of your booking, and send you a copy if you wish. 

  • Why do we have to pay extra when we already have the voucher?

    The first person pays the package price and booking price when checking in, as specified on the website. When you hand in your signed voucher, your companion stays for free. Instead of paying the total sum of the package deal, for example EUR370, you only pay half with the voucher.

  • I would like to log in, but do I have to book immediately after? Will my code expire if I do not book after logging in?

    You can log in to our site as many times as you want before booking. After you have logged in, your code is still valid provided the voucher has not yet expired. The list of hotels and package deals will be on display indefinitely.

  • Can I give the voucher to someone else?

    It is not permitted to sell the LuxuryHotelGiftVoucher to third parties. You can, of course, give it as a present!

  • Are dogs allowed in the hotels?

    You can use the search function on our site to find hotels where dogs are allowed. Some hotels will charge an additional fee. 

  • At what time can I enter my hotel room?

    The check-in times differ from hotel to hotel. Most hotels specify this in the package deal description. If you want to check in earlier, you can indicate this in the comments field of the booking form, and the hotel will contact you.

  • How much do I have to pay extra for a third person in my room?

    The added fee differs from hotel to hotel. Many hotels will place an extra (child’s) bed in your room if requested. If you request this in the comments field, the hotel will contact you about it.

  • How can I change my booking date?

    You can contact us and let us know the new date. We will contact the hotel, and send you a message confirming the alteration.

  • If the booking is rejected, will my code still be valid?

    Should your booking be rejected, your code will be valid again. Please feel free to book anew, even at a later date if you wish.  

  • Can I change my email address as well?

    If you want to use an other email address for correspondence, please pass it on to our customer service. Note: if a booking has been accepted, we can only pass on your new email address to the hotel. We cannot alter your booking request. In case your booking request is rejected, you can book anew with your new email address.

  • What is a city tax?

    A city tax is levied by local authorities on people who do not live in the region where they spend the night. Authorities do this because tourists use local facilities after all.

     

     

     

  • How can I get hold of a voucher?

    The LuxuryHotelGiftVoucher finds its way into the hands of our guests by specially selected associates. You can also keep watching our website www.lhcv.nl for special offers.

  • How long is my voucher valid for?

    The expiry date is listed on the voucher. Usually, the vouchers are valid for one year. If you experience a problem with the validity, you can of course contact us. 

  • Can I use the voucher to pay for VIP packages, booking costs or any other additional costs?

    The voucher cannot be used as currency to pay for any extra costs.

     
  • Can I hand in multiple vouchers?

    You can hand in a maximum of one LuxuryHotelGiftVoucher per two people.

  • Can the voucher be used in combination with other discount offers?

    The LuxuryHotelGiftVoucher cannot be used in combination with other discount offers. The voucher also does not cover taxes, booking costs and cancellation insurance.
